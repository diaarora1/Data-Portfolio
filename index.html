Mission Statement

The purpose of this project is to close the gap between complex information and community action by transforming chaotic datasets into simple, impactful visuals. By utilizing data analysis, I want to turn raw numbers into a path that encourages the next generation to advocate for equality through innovative approaches like data science. I strive to inspire my peers to use analytical tools to uncover systemic barriers and reshape the infrastructure of our future. I aim to prove that data is not just a collection of information, but a powerful tool that turns obstacles into opportunities for growth. Ultimately, I hope to show that through analysis and visualization, we gain the power to build a future that is more inclusive for everyone.
